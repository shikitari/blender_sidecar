Brush Adjuster with Apple Pencil

TL;DR

Want to use Blender with Apple Pencil (macOS + Sidecar + iPad) more comfortably.

My Environment

  • MacBook Pro 2019 macOS Catarina
  • iPad Pro 2018
  • Blender 2.81a / Blender 2.8.2 (I built from source code.)

Features

  • Visualization of pen pressure curve.
  • To sample color with Apple Pencil.
  • To adjust Brush size / strength with Apple Pencil.

How to use

  • Install the Add-on.
  • Edit your keymaps in preferences.
    [Edit] > [Preferences] > [Add-ons] > [Brush Adjuster with Apple Pencil]
  • Press a key you assigned.
  • Touch your screen with your a pen.
  • Adjust it with stroke up-down or stroke right-left
  • Release your a pen.
